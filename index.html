Jordan is a technologist and digital strategist based in Sydney, Australia. He works with government organisations to transform how they work, through organising around user needs, adopting contemporary technology approaches and building digital capability.

He was a founding member of the teams that led the digital transformation of government in the United Kingdom and Australia, and is currently working for the New South Wales Government.

Jordan has also worked on

  • digital strategy and delivery for the Australian Trade and Investment Commission
  • digital trade programs for the UK's Department for International Trade
  • getting young people involved with technology, as a co-founder of Code Week

Jordan previously served as an advisor to former European Commission vice-president Neelie Kroes. In 2018, he was included on the Forbes 30 Under 30 list and occasionally speaks at conferences.
