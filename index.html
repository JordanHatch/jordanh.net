I build interesting things at the Government Digital Service.

Right now, I'm working on GOV.UK, the single website for the government. I've worked on the project since it became a beta.

I'm a member of the Young Advisors Expert Group at the European Commission. I helped a little bit with Europe Code Week.

Last year, the Evening Standard said some nice things about me.

I write week notes (and other things occasionally) on Tumblr.

Hacks

  • Situation Room
    Fetch the status of meeting rooms in the office from Google Calendar. Also see the dashboard part.
  • Departure Lounge
    An airport-style dashboard for Google Calendar. This runs GDS' upcoming releases dashboard.
  • Card-o-matic
    Print out your Pivotal Tracker stories as cards. Code available on GitHub.
  • Anthology
    Anthology is an easier way to keep track of an office or community library. We use it at GDS for our bookshelves.
  • Bowler
    Wrap Foreman to support dependencies and stacks with many processes.
  • hacks.rewiredstate.org
    A database of projects created at Rewired State hack days. Also available on GitHub.
  • Play Your Cards Right
    Play higher or lower with the cost of government websites. Built at National Hack the Government Day 2012.
