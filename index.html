Jordan is a technologist and digital strategist based in Australia. He works with government organisations to transform how they work, through organising around user needs, adopting contemporary technology approaches and building digital capability.

He was a founding member of the teams that led the digital transformation of government in the United Kingdom and Australia.

Jordan has also worked:

  • as an advisor to the Minister for Customer Service in the New South Wales Government
  • on digital strategy and delivery for the Australian Trade and Investment Commission
  • on digital trade programs for the UK's Department for International Trade
  • to get young people involved with technology, as a co-founder of Code Week with the European Commission

Jordan is currently working in the Australian Government on digital reforms to agricultural trade.

In 2018, he was included on the Forbes 30 Under 30 list.

Say hello at jordan@jordanh.net.
